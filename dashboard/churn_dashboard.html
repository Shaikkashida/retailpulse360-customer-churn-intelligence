Insights & Recommended Actions
🔍 AI-Driven Business Insights
Patterns detected from 7,043 customer records
Contract Structure is the #1 Churn Driver
Month-to-month customers churn at 42.7% — 15× higher than two-year contracts (2.8%). This single segment accounts for 87% of total monthly revenue at risk ($120,847). Zero switching cost = low commitment barrier.
First-Year Drop-off: The Onboarding Gap
New customers (0–12 months) churn at 47.7%. Churned customers have an average tenure of just 18 months vs 37.6 months for retained — a 19.6-month gap indicating customers leave before realising full product value.
Fiber Optic Users Churn Despite Premium Pricing
Fiber optic customers churn at 41.9% — the highest of any service tier — despite paying more per month. This is a product-quality signal: high price expectation + inconsistent delivery = churn. DSL users churn at only 19%.
Electronic Check = Payment Friction Proxy
Electronic check users churn at 45.3% vs 15–17% for auto-pay methods. Manual payment customers actively disengage monthly — it's a commitment signal, not just a payment issue.
Annual & 2-Year Contracts: Proof of Concept
One-year contracts churn at 11.3%; two-year at 2.8%. Customers who commit to annual plans are 15× less likely to churn. Migrating even 10% of M2M base to annual protects ~$12K/month.
🎯 Retention Action Plan
Prioritised by revenue impact × implementation effort
P1
Launch "Switch & Save" Campaign
Offer M2M customers with 6–18 months tenure a 12–15% discount to upgrade to an annual contract. Target the 1,200 highest-risk accounts first (high charge + low tenure). Run via email + in-app nudge.
Est. impact: ~$34–38K/month protected at 10% conversion
P1
90-Day Customer Success Program
Proactive check-ins at Day 14, Day 45, Day 90 for all new customers. NPS survey at Day 14 to catch dissatisfied customers early. Fast-track support for customers scoring <7.
Est. impact: 18–22% reduction in Year-1 churn
P2
Auto-Pay Migration Campaign
Offer electronic check users a $5/month discount to migrate to bank transfer or card auto-pay. Reduces payment friction and engagement dropout. Target M2M + e-check overlap first (~900 customers).
Est. impact: 8–10% churn reduction in e-check segment
P2
Fiber Optic Service Quality Audit
Exit surveys with last 200 churned fiber customers. Hypothesis: speed or reliability SLAs not met. Fix: service guarantee + proactive outage notifications + compensation for downtime.
Est. impact: ~$52K/month revenue exposure at 41.9% churn
P3
Senior Citizen Dedicated Support Tier
Senior citizens churn at 41.7% vs 23.6% for non-seniors. Create a dedicated support line, simplified billing, and account management outreach for this segment (1,142 customers).
Est. impact: 15–20% churn reduction in senior segment
