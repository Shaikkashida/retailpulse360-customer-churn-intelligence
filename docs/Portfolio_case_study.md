# RetailPulse 360
Executive Customer Churn Intelligence Platform

# Business Problem
Telecommunication companies lose millions every year due to customer churn.

The objective of this project was to identify the primary churn drivers, quantify revenue at risk, and recommend actionable retention strategies using SQL, Python, R, Power BI, and Streamlit.

# Business Insights
Month-to-month customers show the highest churn.

Electronic Check customers churn significantly more.

Fiber Optic users have elevated churn despite premium pricing.

Customers within their first year contribute most revenue risk.

Long-term contracts dramatically improve retention.

# Recommendations
Convert Month-to-Month customers into Annual Contracts.

Promote Auto Pay migration.

Improve Fiber Service quality.

Launch Early Retention campaigns.

Prioritize High Value Customers.
